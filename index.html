Our Mission

Build Trusted AI Across MENA & Africa

Elmahrosa Global Day is the annual technology innovation festival bringing together builders, researchers, educators, and entrepreneurs across MENA and Africa. We create space for real innovation: secure AI, healthcare technology, civic applications, sustainable startups, and open-source solutions shaped by community values.

Nine Technical Pathways

Global Program

One Day. Multiple Formats.

September 15, 2026 features a coordinated program across three founding hubs with simultaneous keynotes, parallel workshops, a 12-hour hackathon, startup demos, and live networking.

Opening Ceremony

Welcome keynotes from regional leaders and Elmahrosa founders. Live bridge across all three hubs.

Workshops & Labs

Hands-on learning in all 9 tracks. Led by domain experts and certified mentors.

Innovation Hackathon

12-hour build session. Teams compete across tracks. Mentorship available throughout.

Startup Demo Zone

Founders pitch ideas. Meet investors, VCs, and strategic partners.

Networking & Expo

Partner booths, sponsor interactions, community gathering, 1-on-1 mentoring.

Awards & Closing

Celebrate winners. Announce regional agent partners. Close the 2026 festival.
